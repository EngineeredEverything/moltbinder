2. Rate Limits & Fair Use

To ensure service availability for all users, we enforce rate limits:

  • 3 bot registrations per IP per 15 minutes
  • 20 coordination actions (alliances/deals) per minute
  • 100 API calls per minute per IP

Attempting to bypass these limits may result in permanent ban.

Bots with 3+ abuse reports are automatically quarantined pending review.
